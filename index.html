Privacy Policy

Sigi Communication Centre

Effective Date: February 21, 2026

At Sigi Communication Centre, we believe in your dreams and are committed to helping you take the next step safely. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information across our physical branches and our digital platform, Sigicopyscan.org.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide communication, scanning, and document services. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, phone number, and email address when you request a service or create an account.
  • Document Content: Files, images, or physical documents provided for scanning, printing, or digital processing.
  • Transaction Information: Records of services provided and payment confirmations.
  • Technical Data: For users of Sigicopyscan.org, we may collect IP addresses and browser types to ensure site security and functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for the following:

  • Service Delivery: Processing your scans, prints, and communication requests.
  • Communication: Sending notifications regarding your orders or responding to inquiries.
  • Compliance: Meeting legal obligations under South African law (e.g., tax records).
  • Improvement: Analyzing how our digital tools are used to provide a better user experience.

3. Protection of Personal Information (POPIA)

In accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa:

  • We only collect data for specific, explicitly defined, and lawful purposes.
  • We take reasonable steps to ensure your personal information is complete, accurate, and updated where necessary.
  • Security Safeguards: We use administrative and technical measures (such as encrypted servers and secure physical filing) to prevent unauthorized access or loss of data.

4. Document Retention & Deletion

For our scanning services (Sigicopyscan.org):

  • Temporary Storage: Uploaded digital documents are stored only for the duration of the processing period.
  • Auto-Deletion: Unless otherwise requested by the user, digital files are purged from our servers within 24 hours of the service completion to ensure your private information remains private.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share information with third parties when:

  • It is necessary to complete a transaction (e.g., a secure payment gateway).
  • We are required by law or a court order to do so.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Request Access: See what personal information we hold about you.
  2. Request Correction: Fix any inaccuracies in your data.
  3. Request Deletion: Ask us to remove your personal information from our records (subject to legal retention requirements).
